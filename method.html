Curation method

A structured, human-reviewed living database.

Scope

The catalogue tracks Earth observation foundation models, remote-sensing vision-language models, multimodal geospatial models, benchmark papers, datasets, and related tools.

Status labels

seed_verified
Curated detailed entry from the seed workbook. Check latest paper/repo before final academic claims.
seed_specialized
Curated VLM/MLLM entry. Relevant to the landscape but sometimes not a sensor-only EO foundation model.
watchlist_unverified
Registry entry that requires source-paper verification before promotion.
candidate_auto_extracted
Draft entry produced by automation from a submitted URL or paper.
verified
Human-reviewed and approved for the public catalogue.

Recommended academic wording

“We maintained a structured catalogue of EO foundation models and benchmarks using a common schema covering modality, architecture, pretraining objective, scale, downstream tasks, openness, and source availability. Candidate entries were identified through community submissions and scholarly search, then manually reviewed before inclusion.”
