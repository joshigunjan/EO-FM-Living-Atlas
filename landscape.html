How to read this map

The landscape is exploratory, not a ranking. Colors encode release or availability status. Shapes encode the dominant modelling paradigm. The x- and y-axes can be changed interactively to compare modality breadth, modality complexity, task coverage, architecture breadth, openness, or source-link availability.
