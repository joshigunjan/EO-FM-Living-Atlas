How to read this map

The landscape is exploratory, not a ranking. Axis values and marker styles are derived from the catalogue fields: modality tags, downstream-task tags, architecture tags, openness, source links, and the recorded modelling paradigm. Use the task filter to focus on a subset, for example land-cover mapping, change detection, captioning, or regression.
